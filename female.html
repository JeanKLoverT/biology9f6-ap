👗 Fashion Effects on Female Teenagers

📱 Social media shapes what teens see and wear every day. Platforms like TikTok and Instagram can spread fashion trends quickly — but not all trends are healthy to follow.

👖 Wearing Tight Clothes

Popular during “fashionable” days, often linked to confidence + fertility cues.

  • ❌ Skin irritation & pressure marks
  • ❌ Difficulty breathing & movement
  • ❌ Infections from trapped heat & moisture
  • ❌ Disrupted hormonal balance

🥗 Extreme Dieting / “SkinnyTok”

Social media subculture glamorizing very low-calorie diets.

  • ❌ Mental health risks & eating disorders
  • ❌ Hormonal imbalance
  • ❌ Menstrual irregularities
  • ❌ Long-term reproductive issues

✨ What Teens Girls Can Do Instead ✨

Choose looser and breathable clothing to reduce skin irritation, eat balanced meals without following extreme diets, and focus on self-confidence instead of chasing online “skinny” trends.
